Meatballs with Rice

Meatballs with rice are pan-fried minced meat patties, often served with a simple rice side.

They are a hearty and comforting staple of home cooking, known for their savory flavor and tender texture.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef and pork mix
  • Onion, finely grated
  • Bread, soaked in milk
  • Salt and black pepper

Steps

  1. Combine the ground meat, grated onion, squeezed bread, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Form the mixture into oval or round patties.
  3. Pan-fry the patties in oil over medium heat until golden brown and cooked through.
  4. Serve hot with a side of steamed white rice.
